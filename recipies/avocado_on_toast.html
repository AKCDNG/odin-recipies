Avocado On Toast

Man... this is a classic. Super easy, super tasty.

Avocado on toast is an aboslute banger. Top it off with some grinded salt and pepper & you've got yourself a healthy, delicious snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocado's
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Rock Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the avocados and mash them up.
  2. Pop the bread in the toaster and toast for 3 minutes.
  3. Once the toast is done, spread the avocado on the toast.
  4. Season well with rock salt and pepper.
  5. If you want to, add a slight drizzle of Olive Oil.
