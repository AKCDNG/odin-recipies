Fried Egg On Toast

Here is one of my all time favourites. You can never go wrong with a fried egg! As long as you don't overcook it.. Then it can taste very strange. A great addition to this recipe is avocado, so we'll be including it within out recipe.

Frying an egg isn't too difficult, but you have to make sure you time it correctly. Also things like oil spread in the pan can affect the egg taste. Choosing different oil or butter can also change the taste of the egg. Lets get to it!

ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Avocado's
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the avocado's and mash them up in a bowl. The texture should feel spreadable once they are mashed up.
  2. Pop the bread in the toaster. Set the toaster for 3 minutes. Do not start the toaster yet. We are just preparing.
  3. Pop the frying pan on a low - medium heat. Poor a little olive oil around the pan. Do not be afraid to be a little generous with the oil - just so it spreads the pan evenly.
  4. Once the oil has heated up, crack both eggs in the pan. One on each side of the pan if possible. Set a timer for 5 minutes.
  5. Once 1 minute has gone by, start the toaster.
  6. 3 minutes later, the toaster should pop up and your eggs should be nicely cooked. Pop the pan on the lowest heat. Once that is done, spread the mashed avocado on the toast evenly.
  7. Once the timer finishes, turn the hob off. Place your eggs on top of the avocado toast. Season as you wish with salt and pepper.
