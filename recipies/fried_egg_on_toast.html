Fried Egg On Toast

Here is one of my all time favourites. You can never go wrong with a fried egg! As long as you don't overcook it.. Then it can taste very strange. A great addition to this recipe is avocado, so we'll be including it within out recipe.

Frying an egg isn't too difficult, but you have to make sure you time it correctly. Also things like oil spread in the pan can affect the egg taste. Choosing different oil or butter can also change the taste of the egg. Lets get to it!

ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Avocado's
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
