Scrambled Eggs On Toast

Scrambled eggs is an all time favourite dish of mine. It is super simple and easy to make. It takes less than 5 minutes and only requires very few ingredients. It is a great breakfast for yourself, or someone else. Today we'll look at how to make the perfect scrambled eggs on toast. I personally have been making this recipe for around 4 years now. There are some little tips and tricks to making the perfect scrambed eggs - I shall reveal all soon...

Scrambled eggs should feel very soft in the mouth. It is also greatly complimented with roasted cherry tomatoes and mushrooms. Gordon Ramsey has said: "I judge a cook on how good their scrambed eggs are". He is absolutely right. This dish is so simple, yet can feel very tough to cook correctly. Lets get started..

ingredients:

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 2 slices Of Brown/White bread
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • optional: Cherry Tomatoes
