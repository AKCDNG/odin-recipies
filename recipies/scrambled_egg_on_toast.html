Scrambled Eggs On Toast

Scrambled eggs is an all time favourite dish of mine. It is super simple and easy to make. It takes less than 5 minutes and only requires very few ingredients. It is a great breakfast for yourself, or someone else. Today we'll look at how to make the perfect scrambled eggs on toast. I personally have been making this recipe for around 4 years now. There are some little tips and tricks to making the perfect scrambed eggs - I shall reveal all soon...

Scrambled eggs should feel very soft in the mouth. It is also greatly complimented with roasted cherry tomatoes and mushrooms. Gordon Ramsey has said: "I judge a cook on how good their scrambed eggs are". He is absolutely right. This dish is so simple, yet can feel very tough to cook correctly. Lets get started ..

Ingredients:

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 2 slices Of Brown/White bread
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Optional: Cherry Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Crack all 3 eggs into a bowl. Whisk with a fork firmly until the yolks have broken in & the eggs look 'yellow-y'.
  2. Once whisked, prepare your toaster by putting 2 slices of bread in the toaster. Make sure the toaster is set for 3 minutes.
  3. Grab a wooden spatula. This is what you'll be using to move the eggs around in the pan
  4. Set the frying pan to a low - medium heat. Place a knob of butter in there. Allow it to melt, but be sure to make sure it melts evenly around the pan.
  5. Start toasting your bread. At this point, the butter should start to pop. Pour your whisked eggs into the frying pan.
  6. Grab your wooden spatula, start to turn the eggs onto each other in a circular motion. After 5 seconds on the hob, place the pan on your kitchen surface & stir consistently with your spatula.
  7. After it has been off the hob for a few seconds, put it back on. Stir on the hob for 10 seconds, then off for 5 seconds. Repeat.
  8. Your arms will hurt, but you must not stop stiring the eggs on itself. After a couple of minutes you'll start to see the eggs almost solidifying. That is a good sign.
  9. After 3 minutes, your eggs will be done and your toast will be ready. Once you feel the eggs are scrambled, add a small knob of butter on top and mix it in. This will give the scrambled eggs a lovely smooth texture.
  10. Place the eggs on the toast. Do not butter the toast. the butter from the eggs will do that for you. Season with salt & pepper
